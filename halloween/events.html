Events

HalloSCENE - October 27 5:00PM - 8:00PM

HalloSCENE is Scene 75's indoor trick or treat for costumed children 12 and under. During the event game cards will still be available for purchase which means you can still play arcade games as well as enjoy other activities like lazer tag or go-karts while at the indoor trick or treating. You can find the event at www.scene75.com/event/halloscene.

Trick or Treat Carnival - October 30 4:00PM - 8:00PM

Trick or Treat Carnival is a free indoor event with candy, inflatables, costume contest, and concessions. The event is being hosted at Crossroads Community Church.

Spooky Bazaar Craft & Vendor Show - October 29 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM

This craft and vendor show is being held at Lorain Community College and wil have 50+ vendors selling different halloween arts and crafts. Admission to this event is completely free. More information about this event can be found at www.thiscleveland.com.

MCCC Costume Day - October 31

On October 31 students attending the Medina County Career Center can wear a SCHOOL APPROPRIATE costume to school. As costumes must still follow dress code the outfit cannot encorperate any masks.
